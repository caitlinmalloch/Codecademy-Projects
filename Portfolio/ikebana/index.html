The Japanese art of flower arranging

What is Ikebana?

Ikebana is the traditional art of flower arranging in Japan. The art is centuries-old and allows the artist to transform natural elements such as flowers, branches and leaves into an elegant and breath-taking display.

About Us

We are a group of Ikebana enthusiasts practicing Ikenobo design on Vancouver Island. This style of design will allow you to create elegant designs and enter competitions in Vancouver bi-annually. The club events will be taught be a certfied Ikenobo instructor, who will guide you on your learning journey.
